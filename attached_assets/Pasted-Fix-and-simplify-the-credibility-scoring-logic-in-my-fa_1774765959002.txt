Fix and simplify the credibility scoring logic in my fake news detection system.

The current system has conflicting rules and over-corrected logic, causing:

* Real news to be classified as UNCERTAIN
* Subtle fake news to be classified as REAL
* Too many forced conditions (like forcing UNCERTAIN)

GOAL:
Create a balanced, consistent scoring system that:

* correctly classifies real news
* flags fake news
* treats subtle misinformation as UNCERTAIN

---

STEP 1: REMOVE THESE BAD RULES

Remove any rules like:

* "if uncertainty >= X → force UNCERTAIN"
* "if any anomaly → cannot be REAL"

These are too rigid and causing wrong results.

---

STEP 2: USE A PURE SCORING SYSTEM

Start score at 50 (neutral baseline)

Then adjust:

---

POSITIVE SIGNALS (+)

+10 → neutral, factual tone
+10 → structured reporting (multi-paragraph, organized)
+10 → mentions officials / institutions
+5  → responsible uncertainty (e.g. "not yet confirmed", "officials said")

---

NEGATIVE SIGNALS (-)

-15 → sensational words ("BREAKING", "massive destruction", "shocking")
-15 → strong unverified claims ("reportedly", "sources say", "claims that")
-10 → speculation phrases ("may have", "could indicate", "analysts believe")
-10 → exaggerated impact without evidence
-5  → excessive capitalization

---

STEP 3: IMPORTANT DISTINCTION

DO NOT treat all uncertainty as negative.

* Responsible uncertainty = small positive (+5)
* Speculative / rumor language = strong negative (-15)

---

STEP 4: FINAL CLASSIFICATION

After calculating score:

0–39   → FAKE
40–69  → UNCERTAIN
70–100 → REAL

---

STEP 5: CRITICAL RULE (ONLY ONE)

If BOTH:

* strong positive signals AND
* strong negative signals

Then:
→ FINAL = UNCERTAIN

(This handles subtle fake news properly)

---

STEP 6: REMOVE IMAGE DEFAULT BIAS

If no image:
→ DO NOT add default score
→ Use text score only

---

STEP 7: OUTPUT

Return:

* score (0–100)
* label (REAL / FAKE / UNCERTAIN)
* clear explanation of which signals were detected

---

GOAL RESULT:

* Real news → REAL (70–85 range)
* Obvious fake → FAKE (0–30 range)
* Subtle fake → UNCERTAIN (40–65 range)

Keep logic simple, consistent, and explainable.
